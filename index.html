Welcome to House of Fades

Defining Your Style — One Fade at a Time

At House of Fades, we’re more than just a barbershop — we’re a destination for men who value style, confidence, and craft. From classic cuts to the latest fades and beard detailing, our expert barbers blend tradition with precision to deliver sharp results every time.

We’ve created a space where men can relax, unwind, and walk out feeling their best. Whether it’s your weekly cut, a special occasion, or just time to freshen up, we’ve got you covered with a laid-back vibe and top-tier service.

Appointments are recommended to keep wait times short, but walk-ins are always welcome. We also carry a selection of quality grooming products to help you maintain your look between visits.

— The House of Fades Team
